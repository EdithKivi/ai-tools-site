How to choose an AI writing tool

The best writing tool depends on what kind of writing you do most often.

1

Essays and drafts

Use ChatGPT when you need ideas, first drafts, essay support, rewrites, or help understanding how to structure content.

2

Grammar and polishing

Use Grammarly when your main goal is fixing grammar, spelling, clarity, tone, and overall writing quality.

3

Content production

Use Jasper, Copy.ai, or Writesonic when you need blog posts, SEO writing, ad copy, or repeated content creation tasks.
