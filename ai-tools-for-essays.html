How to use AI for essays

Use AI as support for planning, editing, and improving essays, not as a replacement for your own thinking.

1

Start with an outline

Use AI tools to brainstorm thesis ideas, section structure, and the main points you want your essay to cover.

2

Write your own draft

Write the essay in your own words first, then use AI tools to improve clarity, structure, grammar, and flow.

3

Review everything carefully

Always check what AI gives you, make your own edits, and use it to strengthen your writing rather than blindly copying text.
