How to Choose the Right AI Tool

New to AI tools? Start simple. The best AI tool is usually the one that solves one specific problem well and fits your workflow.

1

Choose one problem first

Start with a clear goal such as writing essays, fixing grammar, doing fast research, creating slides, or organizing notes.

2

Pick one tool to test

Use ChatGPT for writing and ideas, Grammarly for editing, Canva for design, or Perplexity for research support.

3

Compare time saved

Ask whether the tool genuinely makes you faster. A good AI tool should reduce effort, not add more friction.
